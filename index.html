About the Game

You are small.
The world is not.

Purpose is an atmospheric exploration game about being present in a world far older and vaster than you. There are no objectives handed to you, no lore dumps, no quest markers. Only the light through ancient ruins, the sound of water somewhere below, and the gentle weight of existing somewhere beautiful.

It is a game about witnessing. About the feeling of standing somewhere that doesn't need you — and finding that enough.

Currently in early development. Built in Godot, with hand-drawn art.
